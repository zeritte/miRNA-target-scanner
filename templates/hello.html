miRNA Target Scanner

this app searchs for the entered miRNA name in diana, mirdb and targetscan. then tries to find the concurrent ones and calculates their average value of targeting. finally, lists them in descending order. if you choose to compare only the best 20 percent of all databases, then it does as commanded. because it is a free server, it may get overloaded and crahsed. In order to prevent that, you have to follow the program and tell it to continue.
